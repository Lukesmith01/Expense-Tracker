How to Use

  1. Enter a Description (e.g., "Food", "Transport").
  2. Enter an Amount:
    • Use positive numbers for income (e.g., 5000).
    • Use negative numbers for expenses (e.g., -2000).
  3. Select a Category (Food, Bills, Shopping, etc.).
  4. Pick a Date for the transaction.
  5. Click Add Transaction → It will appear in the history list.
  6. The app will automatically update:
    • Total Income
    • Total Expenses
    • Your Balance
  7. Click the red X button to delete a transaction.
  8. Use the Filter option to view transactions by month.
  9. Click Export CSV to download your records.
